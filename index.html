How it works. Grip = drag to reorder (works with touch). Tap a rank number to type a new spot — handy for big jumps. Type an ADP in any row to get its value vs your rank. Add tier bands to group players; ranks count players only and skip the bands. Everything is stored in this browser (localStorage), so it survives refreshes and closing the tab — Export CSV for a backup or to drop into a sheet. WR is seeded with our offense-adjusted board; RB/TE/QB start from 2026 consensus — adjust freely.

TPG = 2025 targets per game from Pro Football Reference (regular season); a blank means a 2026 rookie or an injury-shortened 2025 (Nabers' ACL, Mike Evans, Jayden Reed). Y/TGT = yards per target (2025 receiving yards ÷ targets, PFR). TE tab: TPG '25 is last year's targets/game; PROJ '26 forecasts this year via TPG × Volume (team offense tier & pass-lean) × Competition (pass-catchers added/lost) × QB (2026 passer quality/TE-fit). Blanks lack a clean 2025 baseline: a 2026 rookie (Sadiq) or limited 2025 (Likely). Projections are structured estimates, not gospel — the QB/roster assumptions are mine. RB tab: CAR/G / TGT/G / REC are 2025 carries and targets per game and total receptions (PFR); blanks are rookies, players who missed 2025, or deep backs below the stat line. TPRR (targets per route run, 2025), shown on the WR and TE tabs, is how often a player earns a target on the routes he runs. WR values come from the Fantasy Life Utilization Hub and PlayerProfiler tracking baselines, with a few (Nacua, Adams, Waddle, Evans) reconciled to DraftSharks' verified 2025 numbers where they conflicted. The one TE value (Kincaid) is from the DraftSharks 2025 leaderboard, which gates at 150+ routes. A * (Tyson, Tate) marks a college TPRR carried over as a rookie projection, not a measured NFL rate. TPRR is charted differently by each provider, so read it within a position rather than across positions. Stat colors. The three stat cells are tinted green / yellow / red for good / average / below-average, judged per column (a different scale from the PPG offense chip). Green / yellow floors: WR TPG 8 / 6, Y/TGT 9.0 / 7.5, TPRR 0.25 / 0.20; RB CAR/G 14 / 9, TGT/G 4.0 / 2.5, REC 50 / 25; TE targets 6.0 / 4.5. Below the yellow floor is red, and a blank stat stays uncolored.
